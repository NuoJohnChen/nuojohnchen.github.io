I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Computing funded by the National University of Singapore. My supervisor is Prof. Bingsheng He. My research interest lies in Large Language Model (LLM) engineering, particularly in unlocking their potential for practical applications through the lens of algorithms, theory, and frameworks (e.g., optimization techniques, reinforcement learning). I have worked enjoyably as a research assistant in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen with Dr. Benyou Wang. Outside coding, you can find me playing poker and ultimate frisbee.
